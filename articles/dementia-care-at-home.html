Memory-Boosting Activities

Stimulating the brain can help slow cognitive decline. Here are a few ideas:

  • Matching games or simple puzzles
  • Familiar music or songs from their youth
  • Sorting or folding laundry to promote focus and routine
  • Looking through family photo albums
  • Repetition-based memory games or word associations

Routines Provide Comfort

Daily structure helps reduce anxiety and confusion. Meals, rest, and activities should follow a predictable schedule. Even small routines like brushing hair or morning tea can ground the day.

Communication Tips

  • Speak slowly and clearly
  • Use short, simple sentences
  • Allow time to respond
  • Use visual cues or gentle touch
  • Redirect rather than correct
