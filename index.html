Memory-Boosting Activities

Engaging the brain can help slow cognitive decline and bring meaningful connection. Here are a few gentle and enjoyable activities:

  • Simple puzzles or memory games
  • Looking through photo albums together
  • Listening to music from their past
  • Storytelling and reminiscence conversations
  • Matching or sorting objects by colour or category

These may seem simple, but when done with love and patience, they can spark joy and help a person feel grounded and valued.
